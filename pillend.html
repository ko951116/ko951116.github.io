Pillend
Pillend facilitates the storage of pills for a day with the container in the tumbler and also makes you don't forget to take them.

Project Overview

Because the tumblers related to pill on the market are merely intended to store the pill separately, we have developed a tumbler that allows you to take pills with water at once without forgetting to take medicine by placing the pill container between the water bottle and the lid.

Pillend provides convenience for children who have to take medicine by allowing them to take pills with water. It facilitates the storage of pills for a day with the container in the tumbler and also makes you don't forget to take them.

Design Challenge

How might we help people who need to take medication regularly take it easily and comfortably?

The Process

1. Research

Inspiration

In the case of children in lower grade of elementary school, you can see many parents giving them the medicine they need to take. From a parent's point of view, they may be worried about whether their children take medicine well without forgetting and losing it, so we came up with a tumbler to solve this problem.

Problems: What makes children to take a medicine difficult?

1difficulty of distinction

It is difficult for children to distinguish similar-looking pills.

2Absence of water

Water is not always near you when you take medicine. It is difficult to take a pill in the absence of water.

3They are confused whether they took the medicine or not.

Children sometimes forget whether they took the medicine or not. Therefore, there is a risk of duplicate doses of medicine already taken.

2. Design Ideation

Exploring Design Ideas

Step1: How to store water with medicine in a tumbler?

Considerations

  • Water and medicine must be separated.
  • Moisture should not be added to the medicine.

Initial Structure

We decided to put a container for storing pills in a regular tumbler. It is located in the middle of the lid and the water bottle. It is divided into four parts, breakfast, lunch, dinner and blocked parts, and the user will be able to take the pill with water by turning it to the desired part. The bottom of the medicine cabinet is partially pierced, and the top part of the water bottle is all pierced except for some.

3. Design Prototype

Exterior Design

Considering that our target user is children, we thought of a cute character shape. The water bottle part and entrance cap are made of thick silicon, and all other parts are made of light plastic, which is light for children to carry around.

There are symbol icons of morning, lunch, and dinner on the rotating partition. So if you fit each icon under the eyes on the lid, it looks like the character’s mouth and tells you which medicine you take, morning, lunch, or dinner.

Structure

We placed the pill container between the water bottle and the cap. It is a partitioned container where you can divide the pill. The container turns in the direction that the user rotates so that the user can rotate to the desired portion of the pill.

Put the pills in each section of the pill container, then turn the pill container and place the medicine you want to take close to the silicon hole. There is a plate which has a small silicon part which pierced in the shape of a cross so that it can only release water by pressure with the motif of the principle of the heart valve. The bottom part of the tumbler is made of silicon, so you can press it with your hands to get water. Therefore, if you don't press the water bottle, the water doesn't come out and prevents the pill from melting in the moisture.

Only one quarter of the lid part is penetrated and the rest is blocked.

Color/Font




Logo

We made the brand logo considering that our target users are children. Children usually don't like to take medicine. We named the product ‘Pillend’ combining pill and friend, thinking it would be nice for kids to take pills with fun and comfortable feeling like a friend.

4. Final Design

Solution

Pillend provides convenience for children who have to take medicine regularly by allowing them to take pills with water. Pillend facilitates the storage of pills for a day with the container in the tumbler and also makes you easy to take them.

What are we offering?

1It relieves parents' worries.

Parents simply need to put the morning, lunch, and dinner pills in the medicine container so that their child can take the medicine properly outside.

2Take water and pills at once.

You don't need to pack a tumbler and pills. Pillend can store pills and water at the same time, so you can always carry pills and water with you.

3Unique structure

Pillend has a structure in which water comes out only when you press it. Therefore, it is easier and safer for children to take medicine with water.
